How to play with WORDLE

Guess the word generated randomly. Enter letters in each box and click submit button or press the enter key. When the letter that entered in each box is not in the right place, but included in the answer word, the box color will be changed to light yellow. When the right letter in right place, the box color will be light blue. When the letter is not event included in the answer word, the box color will be red. The fewer rounds, the more wins. Now, enjoy WORDLE!

How to play with WORDLE

Guess the word! Enter alphabet in each box and click submit button or press the enter key. When the type and position of the entered alphabet correspond to the answer, the box will be blue. When only the type of alphabet is included in the ansewer, the box will be yellow. When both type and position is wrong, the box will be red. You can change the number of character of the answer. You also can check the answer before you submit your answer if you give up to guess the answer. The player who gets the least round to reach the correct answer is the winner. Now, enjoy WORDLE!
